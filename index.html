The Story of Alora

Our Story — Alora Alora is where fragrance meets time. Born from the blend of Aroma and Ora, we create candles that turn simple moments into unforgettable memories. Our aim is to make every man and woman feel special — by transforming their moments into timeless memories. With unique designs, eco-friendly ingredients, and enchanting scents, Alora is more than just a candle — it’s an experience. Every Alora candle is handcrafted with love, infused with rich essential oils, and designed to complement every space — from cozy homes to elegant gatherings. We believe that scents hold stories, and through every flicker, we invite you to reconnect with joy, nostalgia, and serenity. Light. Breathe. Feel. Let every flame flow with your story.
